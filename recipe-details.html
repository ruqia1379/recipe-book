Qabili palau

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g lamb or beef
  • 1 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • spices: cardamom, cinnamon, cumin, salt

Instructions:

  1. Boil the rice until 80% cooked and drain.
  2. Fry the meat with onions and spices until tender.
  3. Fry the carrots and raisins separately.
  4. Layers rice, meat, carrots and raisins in a pot.
  5. steam on low heat for 20 minutes.
